We’ve been told it is not possible to overachieve our customers’ expectations. We have not reinvented the wheel, we decided to build upon it.

Our service

HackIT has a proven track record of providing world-class security solutions to a diverse range of customers, including Fortune 500 companies, government agencies, and leading organizations across industries.

Security and Consulting Services

HackIT offers comprehensive security and consulting services to help clients identify and mitigate security risks, optimize security posture, and comply with regulatory standards.

Attack Surface Discovery

We use advanced tools and techniques to identify and map the attack surface of our clients' systems, applications, and networks, and provide actionable recommendations to reduce risk

Gap Assessments

Our gap assessment service helps clients identify gaps in their security controls and policies, and develop remediation plans to address them.

Red Teaming

Our red teaming service simulates real-world attacks to test the effectiveness of our clients' security defences, and provides actionable recommendations for improvement.

Training and Capacity Building

We offer customized training and capacity building programs to help clients build strong security cultures and develop the skills and knowledge needed to protect their assets.

Penetration Testing & Vulnerability Assessment

Our vulnerability assessment and penetration testing services help clients identify and prioritize vulnerabilities in their systems, and develop effective remediation plans

Compliance Audits and Regulatory Standards

We help clients navigate complex compliance requirements and regulatory standards, and develop effective strategies for compliance.

Threat Intelligence

Our threat intelligence services provide clients with timely and actionable intelligence on emerging threats and attack trends, helping them stay ahead of the curve.

NOC/SOC Consulting

We help clients design, build, and optimize their security operations centers (SOCs) and network operations centres (NOCs) to improve incident response and reduce risk.

Source Code Review

Our source code review service helps clients identify vulnerabilities and security weaknesses in their software applications, and develop effective remediation plans.

Managed Security Service

We offer fully managed security services that provide clients with 24/7 monitoring, detection, and response to security incidents, as well as ongoing threat intelligence and expert guidance.
